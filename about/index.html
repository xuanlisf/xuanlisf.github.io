Pronounced Schwinn, like the bike

I live in San Francisco and work as a Product Marketer at Lyft. I started this blog as a way to practice some of the web dev concepts I had learned for fun as well as write longer posts about various topics. I admire people who write consistently and are able to clearly articulate a certain idea or give me a glimpse of a different life. I figured this blog could be a good way to improve my writing skills and share some of the projects that I work on.

Some blogs that I’ve really enjoyed in the past and present include:

  • Paul Graham - The standard for tech / startup essays
  • Paul Stamatiou - Intersection of tech / design / photography
  • Danny Choo - Though currently on a break, I loved following him on his entrepreneurship journey
  • Minimally Minimal - Also no longer being updated, but I looked forward to his industrial design thoughts. Also amazing photographs.

I’ve worked in the consumer space for my entire tech career across many roles - business development, growth marketing, product marketing. Given the relative ’newness’ of the product marketing role in tech companies, I hope to write more about my experience to help fellow PMMs and others who may be interested.

Outside of work, I’m interested in many things - fatherhood, Formula 1, stand up comedy, running, street photography, and many more. I enjoy stumbling upon a hobby, researching it and trying to understand why people love it, then falling in love with it myself.

Don’t hesitate to reach out if you have any comments or feedback!
